Pothigai

The Pothigai Superfast Express is
an overnight Superfast train service
operated between Chennai Egmore
and Sengottai via Villupuram, Trichy,
Madurai, Sivakasi and Sankarankovil
on the Southern Railway zone of the
Indian Railways

Nellai express

Nellai Superfast Express is a
Superfast train operated in and
by the Southern Railway zone of
the Indian Railways. It runs on
all days connecting Chennai Egmore with
Tirunelveli Junction via Villupuram,
Trichy and Madurai through the chord line

kaniyakumari

The Chennai Egmore–Kanniyakumari
Superfast Express or Kanyakumari Express
is a Superfast daily overnight train operated
by Southern Railway zone of Indian Railways
that runs between Chennai Egmore and Kanniyakumari
in India. It is currently being operated with
12633/12634 train numbers on a daily
